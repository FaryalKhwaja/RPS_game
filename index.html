Let's Play Rock, Paper, Scissors!!

This game is usually played by humans but we have created a virtual mode of it where you can play it in the console with the computer!!

RULES:

  • You enter a move into the pop up you see on the screen.
  • Make sure you enter either ROCK, PAPER, or SCISSORS!
  • Case does not matter, but spelling does, so check before you enter!

DIRECTIONS:

  • Enter your move into the pop up!
  • The computer will generate it's own!
  • If you click "CANCEL", the game will end and you'll need to restart the game.
  • You will get to play five rounds!
  • The one with the most points win! It's either you or the computer!
  • ENJOY!
