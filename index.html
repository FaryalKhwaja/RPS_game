Rock, Paper, Scissors!!

Traditionally played face-to-face, this game is now available in a virtual format. Challenge the computer right from your console.

RULES:

  • When prompted, enter your move into the pop up. (Rock, Paper, or scissors).
  • Case does't matter, but spelling does, so double-check before you enter!

DIRECTIONS:

  • Type your move into the prompted pop up!
  • The computer will generate it's own move in response!
  • Clicking "cancel" will end the game, refresh to restart.
  • You will get to play five rounds!
  • The one with the most points win! It's either you or the computer!
  • ENJOY!
